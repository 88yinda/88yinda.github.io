OUR STORY

A Legacy of Cross-Border Collaboration

Our Founding Principles

Established in 2005, Bofa Group LLC emerged from a shared vision to bridge the gap between American and Chinese real estate markets. Our founders, with decades of combined experience in both markets, recognized the untapped potential of cross-border collaboration.

What began as a small brokerage facilitating residential investments has evolved into a comprehensive platform managing over $2.8 billion in cross-border real estate transactions, spanning commercial developments, residential complexes, and mixed-use projects.

We've navigated market fluctuations, cultural nuances, and regulatory changes to become the trusted partner for over 500 institutional investors and thousands of individual clients.
